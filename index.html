About me

A bit
about me

Originally from the UK, I moved to the States after falling for my beautiful wife Sarah. We spent some time living in Orlando before heading north to New York with our dog, Fox.

I didn't get to do much mountain biking in the Florida flatlands, so I’m looking forward to hitting some actual hills again. I try to work out mainly because I love good food. I’m a self-taught piano player, a PC gamer, and I have a habit of over-engineering my life through home automation and a home lab. When I can get away from a screen, I’m usually exploring, learning something new, or out in the cold with my camera. My favorite time of year is winter—you can credit Santa or skis, both are valid.
